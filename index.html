About Asha Resins Limited

Asha Resins Limited, established in 1996, is a leading Indian manufacturer of ion exchange resins and specialty chemicals. For over 25 years, we've empowered industrial water treatment across India and globally with high-performance, reliable, and affordable solutions.

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing facility
  • Dedicated in-house R&D lab for continuous innovation
  • Advanced production using German Glass-Lined Reactors (GLRs)
  • Export-ready packaging & international clientele
  • Trusted by 250+ customers including OEMs & sugar mills
