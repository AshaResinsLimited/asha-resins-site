About Us

Established in 1996, Asha Resins Limited is a trusted name in ion exchange resin and water treatment chemical manufacturing. With a state-of-the-art facility near Pune and a strong legacy of quality, we serve customers across India and export to global markets with a focus on performance, reliability, and innovation.
