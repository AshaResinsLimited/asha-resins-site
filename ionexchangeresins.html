Ion Exchange Resins

ASHAION® is the flagship brand of Asha Resins Limited, delivering high-performance Ion Exchange Resins for critical industrial, municipal, and OEM applications since 1996.

Our resin portfolio includes Strong Acid Cation (SAC), Strong Base Anion (SBA), and Mixed Bed resins — all manufactured under ISO 9001:2015 systems with REACH-ready formulations and stringent quality protocols.

Trusted across power plants, water treatment facilities, electronics, refineries, and pharmaceutical industries, ASHAION® resins are known for their consistent performance, customizable grades, and technical support.
