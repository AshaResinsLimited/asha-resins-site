Who We Are

Asha Resins Limited is a pioneering Indian manufacturer of ion exchange resins and specialty water treatment chemicals, serving diverse sectors including sugar, water treatment, pharmaceuticals, refineries, and OEMs since 1996. With our headquarters in Pune and a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Dhanore (Maharashtra), we combine deep industry experience with modern R&D-driven production to deliver high-performance chemical solutions.

Advanced Manufacturing

Equipped with German GLRs, quality control systems, and automation — ensuring precision and scale.

Global Reach

Trusted by clients across the USA, France, Asia, and the Middle East. Expanding rapidly through exports and channel partnerships.

Quality Certifications

ISO 9001:2015 certified with a focus on sustainable, REACH-ready formulations and industry best practices.

Brand Ownership

All products marketed under our own registered brand ASHAION®, with a focus on building long-term equity and trust.

Our Journey

1996

Asha Resins founded by Mr. Kiran Karnawat.

2000

Launched the Sugar Process Chemical Division.

2008

3x capacity expansion with the first plant upgrade.

2015

Second plant expansion, doubling capacity again.

2016

Entered global markets with the first export to a global leader in USA.

2018

Established a comprehensive dealer network across South India.

2024

Transitioned to Asha Resins Limited, reflecting growth and ambition.

2024 & Beyond

Ongoing capacity expansion to 2x current production.
